Popular Communities

Dubai is considered as one of the top places to live in the world. When it comes to living in Dubai, people have thousands of options to consider as their home place. Dubai has many popular communities from the luxury residential area of Dubai Hills to the popular man-made island of Palm Jumeirah, Downtown Dubai and aptly called the 'Centre of Now'.

Apartments in Dubai have been encompassed with modern communities such as gyms, full time security, pools and retail outlets. Locations like Palm Jumeriah, Dubai Marina, Downtown Dubai, and JVC are especially popular due to their location and beautiful surroundings.

Let’s explore the few important apartment communities in Dubai.

Palm Jumeirah

The Palm Jumeirah, also known as 'the Palm, is the world’s largest man-made island. It is possibly one of the most prestigious addresses in the world. With luxury villas, townhouses and apartments, residents enjoy breathtaking sea views of the Arabian Gulf and the iconic Dubai skyline, access to private beaches, and many dining and entertainment options.

Dubai Marina

Dubai Marina, one of the city’s most prestigious waterfront communities, offers an exceptional blend of luxury living and vibrant city life. Most people select it because of its modern amenities and beautiful views and high-rise properties. Everyone can easily connect to all major districts with well-developed road links, trams, and metro. Dubai Marina offers a luxury lifestyle to residents by shopping at Marina Mall and dining at world-class restaurants and bars.

Downtown Dubai

Downtown Dubai is a vibrant hub that offers luxurious living, it is considered as home of renowned landmarks like the Burj Khalifa and Dubai Mall. Residents here experience the perfect blend of convenience with its luxury residences surrounded by premier shopping, fine dining and endless entertainment.

Business Bay

Business Bay is a commercial and residential district that serves as a true cosmopolitan hub in the heart of Dubai. Defined by its modern skyscrapers, stylish apartments, and vibrant retail and dining options. It offers a fast-paced and dynamic lifestyle infused with a Middle Eastern charm. From compact studios to luxurious penthouses, the area caters to a diverse range of residents looking for convenience and complexity.

Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC)

Jumeirah Village Circle, widely known as JVC, is a sought after family-friendly community known for its calm and relaxed atmosphere. With nearly planned streets, modern residential buildings, and green parks, it offers a traditional neighborhood with full range of services and amenities. Recognized for its affordability and strong sense of community, JVC is an ideal choice for those looking for comfort, convenience, and a welcoming environment in Dubai.
