Off-plan apartments?

Off-plan properties refer to properties that are in the planning or under construction stages. Buying an off-plan property in Dubai (UAE) provides many benefits, as many developers provide best incentives during the initial phases that include flexible post-handover payment plans.

Buying off-plan apartments in Dubai before its construction is complete is the best strategy because it allows investors to enter the market at a competitive price point and see significant capital growth.

Studios apartments are in high demand in Dubai. This apartment is the best choice for investors looking for stable income and can be rented both long-term and short-term. Apartments usually have premium infrastructures such as swimming pools, gyms, wellness zones, and kid’s playgrounds.

What makes off-plan apartments more interesting?

The innovation of developers and variety of each project. There are many types of off-plan apartments, from studio apartments designed for couples to 3-bedroom apartments that are designed for families. Off-plan projects feature modern amenities, smart home technologies and luxury communities such as pools, gyms, green areas and retail spaces. These off-plan properties are in prime communities such as Dubai Hills, Jumeriah Village Circle, Dubai Creek Harbour etc., these areas promise future growth and luxury lifestyle.

Why choose off-plan apartments?

By choosing off-plan apartments, buyers secure modern homes but also gain access to long-term wealth opportunities. With Dubai’s investor-friendly policies, high demand for rental returns, development for continuous development of new projects, off-plan apartments provide flexibility, affordability and potential growth.

Off plan apartments are very popular type of property in Dubai and these can be found across most of the city’s communities. This is especially true for new communities where new developments are being released on a regular basis. Some of these include:

  • Dubai Hills Estate
  • Rashid Yachts and Marina
  • Dubai Creek Harbor
  • Jumeirah Village Circle
  • Arjan

Benefits of off-plan apartments

For investors, off-plan apartments represent smart wealth-building opportunity for long-term. These off-plan properties not only benefit during payment appreciation on handover but also provide high rental returns in the city because of high demand for modern housing. This is considered as one of the safest and best investment options in Dubai.

Payment options in off-plan

Flexible payment plans are main advantage for first time buyers with small downpayment options, instalments during construction and post-handover options.
